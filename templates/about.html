About Us

The Terrace is an Interior Design Studio that brings simple design furnitures and simple interior design. By inspiring from the Zen Aesthetics from Japan, which is the art of traditional Japanese gardens, we hope to bring naturalness and simplicity to the interior. Our goal is to bring elements from the nature and embed to the interior design, therefore, it creates a sense of harmony of the nature.
